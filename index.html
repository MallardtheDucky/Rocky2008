Our Plan for American Renewal

We will take bold, immediate action to restore our economy and empower working families. Our plan focuses on three core pillars:

  • Rebuilding the Economy: Launching a National Works Program to create 500,000 jobs, revitalizing our manufacturing sector with the 'Steel Belt Deal', and ensuring "Buy American" is the law of the land for our government.
  • Healthcare & Education for All: Passing the American Health Security Act to create a universal, single-payer healthcare system, while making community college free and funding universal Pre-K.
  • Securing Our Future: Achieving energy independence by 2012 through the America First Energy Act and restoring our nation's leadership in science and technology by doubling NASA's budget.

About John D. Rockefeller V

A Lifetime of Public Service

John D. "Rocky" Rockefeller V has dedicated his life to the belief that government can be a powerful force for good. Born into a family with a long legacy of philanthropy, John learned early that with great privilege comes an even greater responsibility to serve the public good.

Rejecting a career on Wall Street, he chose a path of public service after law school, starting as a community organizer in the struggling industrial towns of the Rust Belt. He saw firsthand the devastating impact of outsourcing and neglect on working families. This experience solidified his unwavering commitment to economic justice and the idea that a strong nation must have a strong middle class.

A Governor for the People

As Governor, John balanced the state budget every year while expanding vital services. He wasn't afraid to take on powerful special interests. He invested in education, creating a free community college program that became a model for the nation. He fought to protect union jobs by vetoing anti-worker legislation and personally negotiating to keep factories open. He also championed landmark environmental legislation, preserving millions of acres of wilderness.

A Leader for Our Times

John is a leader who listens more than he talks and prefers bold ideas to empty talking points. He and his wife, Sarah, a public school teacher, have been married for 25 years and have three children. They understand the hopes and challenges of American families because they are one. Now, John is ready to bring his proven record of results and his unwavering commitment to progress to the White House.

The American Renewal Policy Platform

We will invest in people and infrastructure while maintaining fiscal responsibility through progressive taxation and smart spending. Here's our blueprint.

Economic Recovery & Financial Crisis

"We will provide immediate relief to families, hold Wall Street accountable, and invest in Main Street to rebuild our economy from the ground up."

  • Direct Payments: $300B for immediate relief, with $1,500 per person and $3,000 per family.
  • Aid to States: $100B to prevent layoffs of teachers, firefighters, and police officers.
  • Mortgage Relief: Federal buyout and refinancing of distressed mortgages at a fixed 3% rate.
  • Conditional Bailout: A $500B fund where the government takes equity stakes in bailed-out companies, caps executive pay, and mandates employee representation on boards.
Jobs & The American Worker

"The American worker is the best in the world. We will end unfair trade, invest in manufacturing, and guarantee that every American who wants to work, can work."

  • National Works Program: A "Domestic Civilian Corp" of 500,000 federal jobs in infrastructure.
  • Manufacturing Renaissance: The "Steel Belt Deal" will modernize factories and triple SBA funding for manufacturing loans.
  • "Buy American": Mandate an 80% domestic content requirement for all federal contracts.
  • Worker First Act: Raise minimum wage to $8.50/hr, guarantee 4 weeks paid vacation, and create 500,000 paid apprenticeships.
Healthcare

"Healthcare is a right, not a privilege. We will pass the American Health Security Act to finally guarantee universal healthcare for every citizen."

  • Universal, Single-Payer System: Phased in over 4 years.
  • Immediate Medicaid Expansion: Cover all unemployed individuals and their families.
  • Negotiate Drug Prices: Empower the federal government to lower prescription drug costs.
  • Comprehensive Benefits: Coverage will include mental health, dental, and vision care.
Farms & Rural America

"We will ensure that our family farmers are not just surviving, but thriving, with guaranteed profits and investment in rural communities."

  • Guaranteed Profits: Increase farm price floors by 40% with federal purchase guarantees.
  • Rural Investment: Deliver broadband to every farming community within 2 years.
  • Young Farmers: Provide $25,000 grants for new farmers under 35.
  • "Grow America" Program: Mandate federal contracts for schools and military to use American-grown food.
Energy, Space & The Future

"To win the 21st century, America must lead. We will achieve energy independence and once again reach for the stars."

  • American Energy Corporation: Increase budget to $100B to focus on nuclear power, domestic oil/gas, and solar manufacturing.
  • Energy Independence by 2012: Double the Strategic Petroleum Reserve.
  • Double NASA Budget to $30B: A bold investment to restore American leadership.
  • Ambitious Goals: Establish a moon base by 2012 and a crewed Mars colony by 2020.
